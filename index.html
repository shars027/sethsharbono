Spring 2018
HTML/CSS/Javascript

The assignment was to create a typeface for the alphabet in CSS, and then design a website to showcase the font in use. My font was very meticulously designed with almost 10,000 lines of code written.
[ click on any image to visit website ]

Each letter follows the same 5 guidelines:
- Diagonal lines are thin, with a steep angle
- Horizontal lines are thick with a solid fill
- Vertical lines are also thick but with a transparent fill, and only display the stroke
- Full circles are solid black
- Partial circles have 4 rings with transparent fills and thin strokes

Fall 2016
Multimedia

This was my first assignment when I started at Parsons. The assignment was to create two posters, one for me and one for my partner, and make portraits that communicate how we're each perceived by outsiders (people unfamiliar with us). First I decided to find song lyrics that describe how I interpreted my partner and I. I then took pictures of both of us traced our different features (hair, shirt, skirt, face). Then, I overlaid the lyrics onto those different features. Finally, I printed Each layer out, cut out the negative space, and layered them to create this somewhat 3D/optical image of both of us.
