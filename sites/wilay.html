when I look at you
illustrated book
Assignment/Personal Project — This is easily the most personal, and labor intensive project I've ever worked on. I attended an art high school where I took a book making class my junior year. In that class we made 8-panel accordian books. In my book, I illustrated the metaphors I envision when I listen to my favorite song "When I Look At You" by Miley Cyrus. The song is deeply personal to me and has been my favorite song since I first heard it more than a decade ago.

In College, one of my core classes had an assignment to create a childrens' picture book, and I took this opportunity to remake the illustrations using my more refined new design skills. This time creating a full book with all of the lyrics.

I would never consider myself an illustrator, so as one might imagine; this was a very time consuming project. Since this was a second go-around, it was important to me to do right by the song and the lyrics, and I couldn't be more proud of how this turned out. Although, the images you see here are Photoshop mockups, I did actually get this professionally printed as a keepsake.
