at the movies lp
New Album Release — Sleeve & Label Design

The cover and back cover had already been created by the artist's management with an old-time/classic theatre aesthetic, so for the remainder of the package I continued with that direction. The inner sleeve theatre photo was part of the cover photoshoot, the fonts were pulled from the title treatment used on the jacket, and the film strip composition and the label's film reel were treated to compliment one another.
