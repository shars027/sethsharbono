One of the biggest and longest projects I had the honor to work on at Sony Music, was the 30th Anniversary of Mariah Carey's career and the release of her first album. I was tasked with creating a logo that would brand the campaign to promote her discography, and was used on on all of her singles that were re-released. I subsequently worked on reissuing the first half of her discography on vinyl.
Vinyl Re-Issue — Retouching & Reconstructing Original Package

Like many of these older releases, we did not have the original files for this vinyl package. I recreated the original LP by taking scans of the physical package, recoloring the images to restore color from fading due to age. I retouched each photo to remove dust and scratches, and removed all the typography from the scans. I then reset all of the copy with the original fonts that were used to make sure during this Re-Issue the packages came out look as if they were brand new.
