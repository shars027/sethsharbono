Brand Identity Assignment — Update an existing brand.

My approach to this brand update was to reinvent pharmaceutical packaging for the brand 'Vicks'. The logo revision was influenced by the existing logo's triangular container which informed the shape of the 'V' and lead to the rest of the letters getting a similar 'coated' treatment. The goal was to create a unique identity that stands out playfully in one of the most overwhelming aisles in superstores. If someone is desperate for symptom relief it can be exhausting to sift through shelves with hundreds of products and brands trying to find exactly what they need.
