This was an important project for me, because this was the first full art directed project I got to work on with a frontline artist. A unique challange was presented because we were unable to do any photoshoots in person during the entirety of the project due to COVID-19, so we had to think outside the box. For this first single 'Friends', I went entirely illustrative. This was a very quick turnaround since the label wanted to release the song during such a relevent moment, so I had the artist and the featured artist take selfies for me facetime-style. I then asked Mishaal if he could pose with the ASL sign for "I Love You" since the chorus of the song is "I love my friends." Then I traced over those selfies with a Procreate Brush.
I also animated the Instagram teaser for my first art directed cover.
This is my favorite cover from this project. I'm a huge proponent of branding so it was imperative that I use the same logo and same typeface from the first cover. But for the imagery, we were able to get a friend of Mishaal's to take some portraits for the cover. I illustrated the crown in the same style as the 'Friends' illustration.
The final single that was released prior to the EP drop. This was also the first single that was released with a music video, so the label requested imagery from the music video be used with the established branding.
Finally, the EP cover. The photograph was chosen by the artist. It's an image of his father and was taken by his uncle. I simply added a slight color filter, did some retouching, and added his logo.
